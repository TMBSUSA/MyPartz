Looking for a simple and straightforward way to buy and sell car parts? MyPartz has you covered.

MyPartz is an Australian based marketplace to buy and sell car parts. List your car parts for sale. Browse and buy parts. It really is that simple.


How it works

Search nearby parts

When searching for parts to buy, MyPartz will list search results based on proximity to your location. This helps you find a deal that is nearby and not on the other side of the country. Transact faster and more efficiently!

Add parts to your Garage

So you’ve found some parts you are thinking of buying but you want to save them to get in touch with the sellers later? Add them to your personal garage!

MyPartz makes it really easy to build up a list of parts that you are interested in purchasing. Just add the parts to your garage and get in touch with the sellers whenever it is convenient for you.

Sell your parts for free

There is no commission to sell parts on MyPartz! List your parts for free and keep all the proceeds of the sale!
